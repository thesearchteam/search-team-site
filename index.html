Our Mission

We are a grassroots volunteer team deploying thermal drones and expert search personnel to locate Jack and Lilly Sullivan, missing from Pictou County, Nova Scotia. Our mission is to use modern tools where traditional searches struggle, and to keep hope alive.

How We Search

Using drones equipped with thermal imaging and AI-assisted footage analysis, we can scan large wooded areas quickly and accurately — day or night — helping to spot heat signatures and terrain disturbances that are invisible to the naked eye.

Why This Matters

The official search has paused. The terrain is vast and difficult. But Jack and Lilly are still missing. We believe technology and persistence can make the difference. We are continuing the search independently — and we need your help to keep going.
